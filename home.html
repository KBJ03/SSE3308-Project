About Us
TechTonic Sdn Bhd: Pioneering Excellence in Computer Accessories

Welcome to TechTonic Sdn Bhd, your premier destination for
cutting-edge computer accessories. Established with a vision
to revolutionize the technology landscape, TechTonic has swiftly
grown to become a trusted name in the industry, renowned for our
commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.




Our History

TechTonic Sdn Bhd was founded by Howard Khoo Zi Kang and Lim Yap Yuan with the objective of providing high-quality, affordable computer accessories to tech enthusiasts and businesses.

Our journey began with the opening of our first shop in Taman Greenwich, Kuala Lumpur, where we quickly gained a reputation for our exceptional products and customer service.

As technology and market demands evolved, so we embracing the digital revolution, TechTonic transitioned from a traditional brick-and-mortar store to a comprehensive online shop. This move allowed us to reach a broader audience and offer our products and services more conveniently and efficiently.
